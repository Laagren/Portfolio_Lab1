Academic Interests

Since gaming has been a big part of my life I have always had an interest in computers and tech, which finally led me onto programming.

Over the course of my time at the game dev programme we have programmed alot in C# which I enjoy very much. However, my favourite subject is computer graphics, which we had a course in last year. It was really interesting to learn how games are rendered and how different graphical components work behind the scenes in games and in game engines.
